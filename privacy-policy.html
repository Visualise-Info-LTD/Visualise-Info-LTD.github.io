Privacy Policy

As of August 2025


This privacy policy has been compiled to better serve those who are concerned with how their personally identifiable information (“PII”) is used by Visualise Info Ltd (a company registered in England with number 11723865, and references to “us” and “we” are to that company).
PII is information that can be used on its own or with other information to identify, contact, or locate a single person, or to identify an individual in context.
Please read our privacy policy carefully to get a clear understanding of how we may collect, use, protect or otherwise handle your PII in accordance with our website.

Do we use 'Cookies'?

At Visualise Info, we respect your privacy to the utmost degree. As part of our commitment:

We do not use Session or First-party cookies.
Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your computer's hard drive through your Web browser (if you allow) that enables the site's or service provider's systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information. Given our commitment to your privacy, we have chosen not to use Session or first party cookies at all on our website.

However, some of the products we provide may use cookies to enhance the user experience or provide additional services. We may also use your browser's own cache to store and retrieve user experience data (such as remembering details for future logons), such use can usually be controlled through your device (subject to the way your browser is installed, administration privileges granted to users and your ability to access your own device).

We do not track or store personal information.
We do not collect personal information about your visit to our website. Any data collected is anonymized and used purely for analytical purposes to help improve the user experience.

Collection of Personal Information

While we do not collect personal information through tracking or cookies, we may gather personal data that you provide to us when you fill out a contact form, contact us directly, or engage in transactions or other activities where personal information is required for processing

Use of Your Personal Information

The personal information we collect is used solely for the purpose it was provided and related purposes as permitted by law, such as:

  • Responding to your inquiries
  • Processing transactions
  • Providing you with information about our products and services
  • Sending periodic emails regarding your order or other products and services we have previously discussed
Sharing of Your Personal Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personal information. This does not include website hosting partners and other parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or serving our users, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. We may also release information when its release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others' rights, property, or safety.

Security of Your Personal Information

We take the security of your personal information very seriously. We have implemented security measures to protect the personal information we collect from unauthorized access, use, and disclosure. We also ensure that any third parties with whom we share personal information have appropriate security measures in place.

Your Rights
