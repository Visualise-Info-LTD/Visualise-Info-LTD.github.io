Helios by Visualise Info is a first-of-its-kind software platform that unites Internet of Things (IoT) sensor data, Enterprise Asset Management (EAM) systems, and 3D digital twin technology into a single intuitive interface. The result is clear, context-rich insights delivered in real time, turning raw data into the right decisions at the right moment.

Helios works with IBM, SalesForce, SAP, IFS and more...

At Visualise Info, we bridge the gap between complex enterprise systems and the people who use them. Our visual-first platform lets you navigate your physical environment, access data in context, and take action, all through an intuitive 3D interface.

Key Advantages of Helios

Designed from the ground up for visual asset management, Helios offers unique capabilities that set it apart from traditional solutions.

  • First-of-its-kind Platform

    The only platform purpose-built to visualise both enterprise asset data and IoT data together in one place.

  • IoT + 3D Integration

    Helios overlays live IoT sensor readings onto interactive 3D digital twins, enabling proactive monitoring and rapid root cause analysis (RCA).

  • Future-Proofed EAM

    Use real-time data and root cause analysis to anticipate issues before they escalate, reducing downtime and maintenance costs.

  • Context-Aware Intelligence

    Helios dynamically adjusts the data displayed based on each user's location and role. Field engineers, inspectors, and executives each see exactly what they need, in context and in real time.

  • Event Storm Management

    Built-in tools cut through alert noise during “event storms” (sudden floods of sensor alerts), isolating the critical issues and surfacing only actionable insights for predictive maintenance at scale.

How it Works.

Helios creates a clear, intuitive data journey, taking you from the initial sensor signal to a actionable visual insight in three simple steps:

  • Ingest.

    Harvests live data from IoT networks and sensors in the field.

  • Process.

    Collects, collates, normalises, and enriches the data in real time.

  • Visualise.

    Delivers geo-referenced insights directly into the 3D View, so mobile teams and executives alike can act with confidence.

  • The Result.

    A truly mobile, context-aware experience that bridges the gap between data and decision-making.
